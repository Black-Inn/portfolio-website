About us

We're the Black-Inn team. We create assets for Minecraft Java/Bedrock in Blockbench, from creatures and props to furniture packs and themed item sets. Our focus are clean shapes, 1:1 product as the customer's vision, optimized presentation, and visuals that feel good in-game.

Usually all we need to get started on your Project/Comission are a couple of references and as for the payment that can always be negociated. We believe a good product deserves a fair price, that way everyone is happy.
